Interest rates can have a significant impact on the value of the US dollar in the forex market. When the Federal Reserve raises interest rates, it makes the US dollar more attractive to foreign investors, as they can earn higher returns on their investments. This increased demand for the US dollar can lead to an appreciation of its value relative to other currencies. Conversely, when the Federal Reserve lowers interest rates, it makes the US dollar less attractive to foreign investors, as they can earn lower returns on their investments. This decreased demand for the US dollar can lead to a depreciation of its value relative to other currencies.
